Registration

You can download FCU from http://fcu.smibe.com. For the evaluation copy, a 30 day trial password will be send automatically to the E-mail address specified in the registration form. The password is required for running FCU. After 30 days a “nag dialog box” will appear every now and then remembering that your copy is not registered.

For a small fee you can register FCU. As a registered user the “nag dialog” won‚t be displayed again. There are two exceptions, in which case you don‚t have to pay the registration fee:

  1. You are using FCU exclusively for non-commercial purposes (you could be a student or developer of freeware products). Just write an e-mail to fcu@smibe.com telling for what you are using FCU.
  2. You have an income below $ 20.000/year, just let me know via e-mail (fcu@smibe.com) and I'll send you a registration password.

If you are part of a buisness and wish to use several copies of FCU reduced site licensing rates are also available!
